About us

Devin Kandola has always had a passion for pizza. It's been his favorite food his entire life, he had such a strong passion for pizza he wanted to make it his career. So he decided to open King of Pizza in 1977 in Surrey, BC. He wanted the restaurant to be a family restaurant with a good vibe.
