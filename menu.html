About Us

King of Pizza has been going strong for over thirty plus years. We opened up in 1983 in Surrey, BC, this was all a dream for Devin Kandola, he's always had a passion for pizza. He loved this passion so much that he wanted it to be his career. He's always wanted his food to be good quality but at a very low price.

We have been awarded the best reastaurant in British Columbia in 1988, 1992, 1994, 1997, 1999, 2001, 2006, 2010, 2016, 2017. We have become a world wide success because for our top notch food. We have people coming from all around the world dieing to eat at King of Pizza.
